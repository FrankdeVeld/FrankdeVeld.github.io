Frank de Veld

Hi! I'm Frank de Veld. I am currently a third year PhD student at Université Côte d'Azur, but working mostly with INRIA and Thales Alenia Space. My research topic is optimal control for satellite collision avoidance, and I have research experience in trajectory optimisation, orbital dynamics, solar sailing and numerical modelling as well. Apart from these reather technical topics, I like to write blog articles about topics like space sustainability and space exploration.
